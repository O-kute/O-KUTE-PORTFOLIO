Biography

My name is Joseph Okute, and I am a dedicated student pursuing a professional career in nutrition. I have always been passionate about understanding how food, health, and human biology connect, and this interest naturally led me into the field of nutrition science. As someone who values wellness and community health, I am committed to gaining the knowledge and skills needed to make a meaningful impact in people's lives.

This environment motivated me to study nutrition so I can contribute to better health outcomes, both locally and beyond. Through my studies, I have developed a strong appreciation for evidence-based practice and the importance of educating communities about healthy living.

Looking ahead, my goal is to become a highly skilled nutrition professional who can work in clinical settings, community programs, or research. I hope to specialize in areas such as diet therapy, public health nutrition, or food science. Ultimately, I want to use my expertise to promote healthier lifestyles, support vulnerable populations, and contribute to building a society where nutrition is understood, valued, and accessible to all.

Nutrition Data Visualization Dashboard

Problem Solved: Many community members lacked clear, accessible information about their daily nutritional intake. The project aimed to simplify nutrition data and present it in a visual format that anyone could understand.

Technology Used: Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets, Basic Data Visualization Tools, Simple UI Layout Design
